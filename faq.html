What makes LVC different from other dating apps?

LVC uses advanced AI-powered compatibility matching based on numerology algorithms combined with real-life data like location, interests, lifestyle, and career goals. Your birthday creates a unique compatibility code while staying completely private - only your match score is shared.

How does the AI matching algorithm work?

Our proprietary algorithm converts your birthday into a powerful compatibility code using numerology principles. This core score is then enhanced with real-life factors like shared location, interests, lifestyle preferences, and career goals to create your personalized match percentage.

Is my birthday information safe and private?

Absolutely. Your actual birthday is never shared with other users or stored in an identifiable format. Only your personalized match score is visible to potential matches, ensuring complete privacy protection.

How many match requests can I send with the free plan?

With our free plan, you can send up to 5 match requests per day. This allows you to explore meaningful connections without overwhelming yourself or others.

What's included in the Premium subscription for $20/month?

Premium includes unlimited match requests, advanced profile customization, enhanced AI matching, priority profile visibility, unlimited messaging, read receipts, advanced privacy controls, and premium support.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Premium subscription at any time. There are no long-term commitments or cancellation fees. You'll continue to have access to Premium features until the end of your billing cycle.

How accurate is the AI compatibility scoring?

Our AI compatibility system combines numerological principles with machine learning algorithms and real-world data points. While compatibility is ultimately subjective, our system provides data-informed insights to help identify potentially meaningful connections.

What information do I need to provide for matching?

To get started, you'll provide your birthday (kept private), location, interests, lifestyle preferences, and career information. This data helps create accurate compatibility scores and meaningful match suggestions.

Is LVC available on both iOS and Android?

Yes, LVC is built with Flutter technology, ensuring native performance and consistent experience across iOS and Android platforms, with responsive web support as well.

How does messaging work on LVC?

LVC includes real-time messaging with multimedia support. Free users get basic messaging features, while Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited messaging, read receipts, and enhanced communication tools.

Can I change my location or other profile details?

Yes, you can update your location, interests, lifestyle preferences, and other profile information at any time. This helps ensure your matches remain relevant as your life evolves.

What privacy controls are available?

LVC offers comprehensive privacy controls including profile visibility settings, blocking and reporting features, and advanced privacy controls for Premium users. Your data is protected with enterprise-grade security.

How do I know if someone is interested in me?

When someone sends you a match request or when there's mutual interest, you'll receive notifications. Premium users get priority visibility and enhanced notification features.

What happens if I don't find good matches?

Our AI continuously learns and improves match quality. You can also refine your preferences, update your profile, or contact our support team for personalized assistance in optimizing your profile.

Is there customer support available?

Yes, we provide customer support through in-app messaging and email. Premium subscribers receive priority support with faster response times and dedicated assistance.
